Producers can promote one or more of their beats to gain increased visibility.
Promoted beats can appear on the landing page on initial page load and be marked as sponsored in beat search results.
Only authenticated producers can promote their own beats.

Promoting a beat requires payment.
Producers must have at least one valid saved payment method before initiating a promotion.
The system must charge the producer using the selected payment method before activating the promotion.

Promotion status must have a defined duration and expiration.
Expired promotions must no longer appear as sponsored.
The system must clearly distinguish promoted beats from organic results.

Promoted beats must not bypass standard licensing, pricing, or purchase rules.
