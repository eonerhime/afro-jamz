Buyers can purchase beats only when authenticated.
Buyers must have at least one valid saved payment method before completing a purchase.
When purchasing a beat, a buyer must explicitly select one licensing term.

The selected license determines the final price and usage rights.
The buyer must select a saved payment method at checkout.
Purchased beats must not be downloadable before successful payment.

After purchase, buyers can access the beat according to the selected license terms.
Purchase history must be recorded and accessible to the buyer.
